Our Project & Aims

Our platform provides intelligent forecasting for pharmaceutical demand, helping businesses optimize inventory and pricing decisions. Key features include:
✅ Formula Tool – Generate predictive insights using advanced models.
✅ Interactive Dashboard – Analyze buyer demographics (age, purchase history, etc.).
✅ Daily & Historical News – Stay updated on pharmaceutical trends and market shifts.
✅ Top-Selling Drugs Insights – Discover the most sold and in-demand medicines of 2022.
